## NOIZE REP PROGRAM — OVERVIEW (Draft)

### 1. PROGRAM OVERVIEW
The NOIZE REP Program empowers users to earn rewards by helping promote music, artists, and
causes featured on NOIZE.music. It is our way of creating an engaged, value-driven listener
economy.

### 2. HOW IT WORKS
Users must subscribe to a premium plan to participate. Once activated, they can earn based on:
- Listening to and sharing songs
- Driving new signups
- Supporting artist campaigns
- Participating in NGO promotions
- Curating or boosting trending playlists

### 3. REWARD SYSTEM
Earnings can be generated through:
- Cash rewards based on streaming performance
- Badges and digital goods
- In-app credits for merchandise or subscription discounts
- Priority access to artist merch drops and beta features

- Dashboard shows daily and monthly earnings
- Payouts processed via Stripe, Razorpay, or international wallets

Users may not use bots, spam, or fake accounts. Violation may result in permanent ban and loss of

Program is open worldwide with regional compliance and taxation aligned accordingly.

NOIZE.music may alter or discontinue the REP Program at any time, with 30 days' notice to users.
